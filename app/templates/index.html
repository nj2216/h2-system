Key Features

Duty & Discipline

About H2 Department

OM SRI SAI RAM. The H2 (Health & Hostel) Department functions with the spirit of service, providing 24/7 healthcare support to hostel inmates.

The primary objective of the department is to ensure that hostel inmates are always healthy — physically and mentally — through organized medical care and disciplined service.

  • Medical Care: First aid for injuries, regular doctor consultations, and medicine administration as prescribed
  • Care for Sick Students: Continuous monitoring of sick inmates and coordination of dietary and medical needs
  • Leave Authorization: Issuing sick leaves only with due permission from the warden and department in charge
  • Records & Stockkeeping: Maintaining medicine record books, stocktaking, and expiry monitoring
